RPG ADVENTURE STORY TRANSCRIPTS
================================

This folder contains saved story transcripts from your RPG adventures.

Each story is saved as a text file with the format:
  [playername]_[universe]_[timestamp].txt

Example:
  pavan_horror_20251129_153045.txt

Stories are automatically saved when:
- The adventure ends (victory or defeat)
- You reach turn 4-5
- You say "save story" or "finish adventure"

You can open these files with any text editor to read your adventures!
