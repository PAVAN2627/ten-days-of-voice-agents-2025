# Background Music for RPG Game

Place your MP3 files here with these exact names:

- fantasy.mp3       - For Fantasy universe (magical, orchestral)
- horror.mp3        - For Horror universe (dark, suspenseful)
- space_opera.mp3   - For Space Opera universe (sci-fi, electronic)
- cyberpunk.mp3     - For Cyberpunk universe (synth, futuristic)
- post_apocalypse.mp3 - For Post-Apocalypse universe (gritty, tense)

The music will:
- Start playing when the game begins
- Loop continuously
- Play at low volume (20%) so it doesn't interfere with voice
- Fade in smoothly
- Stop when the game ends

Recommended music style:
- Fantasy: Orchestral, magical, adventure themes
- Horror: Dark ambient, suspenseful, eerie sounds
- Space Opera: Electronic, sci-fi, space themes
- Cyberpunk: Synthwave, electronic, neon vibes
- Post-Apocalypse: Industrial, tense, survival themes

Music should be:
- Instrumental (no vocals)
- Loopable (seamless loop)
- Not too loud or distracting
- Atmospheric and immersive
